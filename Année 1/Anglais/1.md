# Intro

We're preparing a 2 year HND major in IT. We focus on professional ability and practical skills in English for IT contexts in this course.

We want to be able to communicate effectively, clearly and impactfully in English in professional IT settings.

KISS: Keep It Short and Simple/Keep It Stupidly Simple.

## Grammar and Vocabulary
